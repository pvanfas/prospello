Refund Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

At 360 Marketing School ("we", "us", "our", or "Company"), we want you to be completely satisfied with your enrollment in our programs. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be granted for our courses, programs, and services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this policy carefully before making a payment. By enrolling in our programs and making a payment, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Refund Policy.

1. General Refund Policy

All fees paid for our Services are generally non-refundable unless otherwise stated in this policy or required by applicable law. We provide refunds only in the specific circumstances outlined below and subject to the terms and conditions described.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Refund Requests Within 7 Days: You may be eligible for a full refund if you submit a written refund request within 7 calendar days from the date of enrollment, provided that:

  • You have not accessed more than 20% of the course content
  • You have not downloaded any course materials
  • No live sessions have been conducted or attended
  • The refund request is made before the program start date (if applicable)

2.2 Refund Requests After 7 Days: After 7 calendar days from enrollment, refunds may be considered only under exceptional circumstances, such as:

  • Medical emergencies with proper documentation
  • Unforeseen circumstances preventing course completion (with valid proof)
  • Technical issues on our part that prevent access to the course
  • Course cancellation by 360 Marketing School

2.3 Non-Refundable Items: The following are non-refundable under any circumstances:

  • Application fees or processing fees
  • Fees for courses that have been completed or for which certificates have been issued
  • Downloaded course materials or resources
  • Third-party service fees (payment gateway charges, etc.)

3. Partial Refunds

3.1 Partial Course Completion: If you have accessed more than 20% but less than 50% of the course content, you may be eligible for a partial refund at our discretion, typically:

  • 50% refund if request is made within 14 days of enrollment
  • 25% refund if request is made within 30 days of enrollment
  • No refund after 30 days from enrollment date

3.2 Payment Plans: For students on payment plans:

  • Refunds are calculated based on payments made up to the refund request date
  • Future installments are cancelled upon refund approval
  • Refund processing fees may apply

4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, you must:

  1. Submit a written refund request via email to info@360marketing.school or through our Contact Us page
  2. Include your full name, enrollment ID, course/program name, and reason for refund request
  3. Provide any supporting documentation (medical certificates, etc.) if applicable
  4. Allow 5-10 business days for review and processing

Refund requests submitted through any other method (phone, social media, etc.) will not be processed. You must use the official channels mentioned above.

5. Refund Processing Time

5.1 Review Period: Refund requests are reviewed within 5-10 business days from the date of submission. We may contact you for additional information during this period.

5.2 Processing Time: Once approved, refunds are processed within 10-15 business days. The time taken for the refund to reflect in your account depends on your bank or payment method provider.

5.3 Refund Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the transaction. If the original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to find an alternative method.

6. Refund Amount Calculation

The refund amount will be calculated as follows:

  • Full Refund: Original amount paid minus any non-refundable fees (processing fees, third-party charges)
  • Partial Refund: Calculated based on the percentage of course accessed and time elapsed
  • Processing Fees: Payment gateway charges and processing fees are non-refundable and will be deducted from the refund amount

7. Course Cancellation by 360 Marketing School

If we cancel a course or program for any reason:

  • You will receive a full refund of all fees paid
  • You will be notified via email and other communication channels
  • Refunds will be processed within 15 business days of cancellation
  • We will make reasonable efforts to offer alternative course options

8. Special Circumstances

8.1 Medical Emergencies: Refund requests due to medical emergencies require:

  • Valid medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner
  • Medical certificate dated within 30 days of the refund request
  • Proof of relationship if the emergency is for a family member

8.2 Technical Issues: If you experience technical issues preventing access:

  • Contact our support team immediately to resolve the issue
  • If the issue cannot be resolved, we will provide a full or partial refund
  • Refund eligibility is determined on a case-by-case basis

8.3 Job Relocation or Other Personal Reasons: These are generally not eligible for refunds unless the request is made within the initial refund period and meets other eligibility criteria.

9. Transfer of Enrollment

Instead of a refund, you may be able to:

  • Transfer to Another Batch: Transfer your enrollment to a future batch of the same course (subject to availability and fees difference, if any)
  • Transfer to Another Course: Transfer to a different course (subject to course availability and price difference)
  • Transfer to Another Person: Transfer your enrollment to another eligible individual (subject to approval and transfer fees, if applicable)

Transfer requests must be submitted in writing and are subject to our approval. Transfer fees may apply.

10. Refund Denial

Refund requests may be denied in the following circumstances:

  • Request is made after the specified time limits
  • More than 50% of the course content has been accessed
  • Certificate has been issued
  • Violation of our Terms and Conditions or academic policies
  • Fraudulent or abusive refund requests
  • Insufficient documentation for special circumstances

If your refund request is denied, we will provide a written explanation of the reason for denial.

11. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider:

  • Your account may be suspended pending resolution
  • We reserve the right to dispute the chargeback with supporting documentation
  • Chargeback fees may be deducted from any refund if the chargeback is resolved in your favor
  • We encourage resolving issues directly with us before initiating chargebacks

12. Government Regulations

This refund policy is subject to applicable laws and regulations in India. If local consumer protection laws provide greater refund rights, those laws will take precedence over this policy.

13. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. The refund policy applicable to your enrollment will be the policy in effect at the time of your enrollment. For significant changes, we will notify enrolled students via email.

14. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: info@360marketing.school
  • Website: Contact Us Page

Please include "Refund Request" in the subject line of your email for faster processing.

15. Acknowledgment

By enrolling in our programs and making a payment, you acknowledge that:

  • You have read and understood this Refund Policy
  • You agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this policy
  • You understand that refunds are subject to the eligibility criteria outlined above
  • You will follow the proper refund request process

For questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms and Conditions.
