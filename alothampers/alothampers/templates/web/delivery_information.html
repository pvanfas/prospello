Delivery Information

Thank you for choosing Alot Hampers for your gifting needs in the UAE. We're dedicated to ensuring that your gifts are delivered promptly and with the utmost care. Below, you'll find important delivery information to make your experience seamless:

Delivery Areas: We offer delivery across the United Arab Emirates, including major cities like Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah, and Umm Al Quwain.

Delivery Timings: Our delivery timings are scheduled between 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM local time, seven days a week, including holidays.

Same-Day Delivery: We understand the importance of timely delivery. For orders placed before 12:00 PM local time, we offer same-day delivery within the UAE. Orders placed after 12:00 PM will be delivered the next business day.

Delivery Charges: We provide complimentary delivery for orders above AED 200 within the UAE. For orders below AED 200, a nominal delivery fee of AED 20 will be applied.

Delivery Tracking: Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email or SMS containing a tracking link. You can use this link to monitor the real-time status of your delivery.

Recipient Information: Please ensure that the recipient's contact information provided at the time of ordering is accurate to facilitate smooth delivery. In case the recipient is unavailable at the provided address, our delivery personnel will make reasonable attempts to contact them.

Special Instructions: Should you have any special delivery instructions or requests, such as specific delivery times or preferred packaging, please mention them while placing your order. We'll do our best to accommodate your preferences.

Contact Us: For any inquiries regarding your delivery or to make changes to your order, please feel free to reach out to our customer service team. You can contact us via phone, email, or live chat support on our website.

At Alot Hampers, we strive to exceed your expectations with every delivery. Thank you for entrusting us with your gifting needs in the UAE.
