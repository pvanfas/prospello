ABOUT US

Innovative solutions for a digital world

Headquartered in the vibrant business hub of UAE- Dubai, is a dynamic and forward-thinking entity dedicated to excellence in international trade (Import and export) with a specialized focus on Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) and Established with a vision to provide unparalleled solutions. Limra has emerged as a key player in the global market with wide range of high quality FMCG goods to Africa, Asia and the Middle East. The Company supplies products from all over the world including Malaysia, Indonesia, China, India, Philippines, Europe and the USA.

In 2017, our journey has been marked by a commitment to delivering high-quality FMCG products and unparalleled service. With a dynamic team of industry experts and a strong team on distribution network, we have successfully built a reputation for reliability, efficiency, and the ability to meet the diverse needs of our clients. We engage in wholesale and retail of Personal Care, Food and Beverages, Cleaning products, Baby & Child Care and Paper Products. We cover almost all major cities in UAE through our Distribution Network. We work together with distinguished brands, both locally and internationally, to ensure our customers have access to a wide range of high-quality products that cater to their choice and requirements.

Our base is built on robust collaboration. We have established long-term relationships with major suppliers and manufacturers, enabling us to guarantee a smooth supply chain and deliver products consistently and efficiently. Our comprehensive network allows us to source FMCG products from international markets, and we make sure that we provide industry trends to our customers with the latest products in demand. As a future-oriented company, we continuously invest in market research, innovation, and technology to stay at the forefront of the FMCG industry. Our flexible strategy allows us to adapt high speed market dynamics, anticipate customer requirement, and provide customized solutions that drive business growth.
