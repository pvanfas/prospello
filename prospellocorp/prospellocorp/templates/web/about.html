Prospello Charitable Foundation

Prospello Charitable Foundation is a non-profit community founded by Prospello global group of companies by collaborating with different institutions in providing social service and aids to the needy. It has a comprehensive development approach, Its prime objective is to create and support meaningful activities through innovative institutions to address some of the society’s most pressing developmental challenges The Foundation lays significant emphasis on leveraging technology for developmental solutions. Following its model of direct engagement, and support the Foundation directly reaches out to the affected people through trained volunteers and representatives.
