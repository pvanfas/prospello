Our Story

De L!MRA is a leading construction company of building and construction. De L!MRA is a moving story in the realty sector. Introducing quality control into every detail of our projects, we slowly moved from strength to strength with back-to-back projects. As time ticked into decades of engineering experience, our business intelligence, innovation and investment of resources proved to be going well with each other.
