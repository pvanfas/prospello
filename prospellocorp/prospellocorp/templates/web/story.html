Our Story

From Classroom to Global Impact

Propello's story is one of defying expectations. It began in a high school classroom, where a group of friends, fueled by youthful ambition and a shared dream, embarked on their first entrepreneurial venture. Those early endeavors, while filled with youthful exuberance and inevitable missteps, instilled invaluable lessons – the importance of resilience, the power of adaptation, and the unwavering pursuit of excellence.

Undeterred by early challenges, we embarked on a journey of continuous learning and relentless growth. What began as a single venture has evolved into a diversified group of companies, each a testament to our entrepreneurial spirit and unwavering commitment to innovation.

Today, Propello is a global entity, with a presence across multiple continents and oﬃces spanning major cities. We operate in diverse sectors, from technology and healthcare to finance and consumer goods, constantly seeking new opportunities and pushing the boundaries of what's possible. Our journey is a testament to the power of human ingenuity, the importance of embracing change, and the unwavering belief that with passion, perseverance, and a collaborative spirit, even the most ambitious dreams can become a reality.

Prospello Charitable Foundation

Prospello Charitable Foundation is a non-profit community founded by Prospello global group of companies by collaborating with different institutions in providing social service and aids to the needy. It has a comprehensive development approach, Its prime objective is to create and support meaningful activities through innovative institutions to address some of the society’s most pressing developmental challenges The Foundation lays significant emphasis on leveraging technology for developmental solutions. Following its model of direct engagement, and support the Foundation directly reaches out to the affected people through trained volunteers and representatives.
