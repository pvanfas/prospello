Enrollment & Admissions

Our programme is designed for anyone passionate about marketing, including:

  • Aspiring marketers looking to start their career
  • Students and recent graduates wanting industry-relevant skills
  • Working professionals seeking to upskill or transition into marketing
  • Business owners wanting to master marketing for their business
  • Freelancers and entrepreneurs looking to expand their capabilities

No prior marketing experience is required. We start from the fundamentals and build up to advanced strategies.

Applying is simple! You can:

  1. Fill out the enrollment form on our website
  2. Schedule a free consultation call with our team
  3. Complete the application process (including any required documents)
  4. Receive confirmation and payment details

Our admissions team will guide you through each step and answer any questions you may have. You can also contact us directly via phone or email for personalized assistance.

Our programme has minimal entry requirements to make quality marketing education accessible:

  • High school diploma or equivalent (minimum education)
  • Basic computer literacy and internet access
  • Commitment to complete the 6-month programme
  • Passion for marketing and willingness to learn

We believe in potential and dedication over formal qualifications. During the consultation, we'll assess your fit and help determine if this programme is right for you.

We run multiple cohorts throughout the year. While we have intake periods, we recommend applying early as seats fill up quickly. Our cohorts typically start every few months, and we can inform you about upcoming batch dates during your consultation.

Early enrollment often comes with benefits like access to pre-programme materials and networking opportunities with fellow learners.

Course Details

The Digital Marketing Specialisation Programme is a 6-month intensive course delivered in a flexible online format. This allows you to:

  • Learn at your own pace while following a structured curriculum
  • Attend live sessions and access recorded content
  • Balance learning with work or other commitments
  • Access course materials 24/7 from anywhere

You can expect approximately 10-15 hours of learning per week, including live sessions, assignments, and project work.

Our comprehensive curriculum covers the entire digital marketing spectrum:

  • Marketing Fundamentals: Consumer psychology, brand building, marketing strategy
  • Content Marketing: Content creation, storytelling, content strategy
  • Social Media Marketing: Platform strategies, community management, social advertising
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO): On-page/off-page SEO, technical SEO, local SEO
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC): Google Ads, Facebook Ads, display advertising
  • Email Marketing: Automation, segmentation, campaign optimization
  • Analytics & Measurement: Google Analytics, data interpretation, ROI tracking
  • Marketing Automation: CRM tools, workflow automation
  • E-commerce Marketing: Online store optimization, conversion strategies

Plus hands-on projects using industry-standard tools and certifications.

Yes! Upon successful completion, you'll receive:

  • 360 Marketing School Certificate of Completion - validating your comprehensive digital marketing expertise
  • Industry Tool Certifications - including Google Analytics, Google Ads, Facebook Blueprint, and more
  • Project Portfolio - real-world projects demonstrating your skills

These certifications are recognized by employers and can significantly boost your career prospects. You'll also have a professional portfolio to showcase during job interviews.

You'll gain hands-on experience with industry-leading tools including:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, social media insights
  • Advertising: Google Ads, Facebook Ads Manager, LinkedIn Ads
  • SEO Tools: Google Search Console, SEMrush, Ahrefs (overview)
  • Content: Canva, video editing tools, content management systems
  • Email Marketing: Mailchimp, Sendinblue, marketing automation platforms
  • Social Media: Hootsuite, Buffer, native platform tools
  • CRM: HubSpot, Salesforce (overview)

We provide access to many of these tools during the programme, ensuring you learn with real platforms used in the industry.
